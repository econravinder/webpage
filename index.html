About Me

I am a doctoral student at the Economics and Planning Unit, Indian Statistical Institute, Delhi Centre. My research interests focus on empirical Development Macroeconomics in the areas of Education, Labor, and the Rural Economy. I am currently working on the project: Spatial Distribution of Tertiary Education Institutions and Attainment in Rural Rajasthan.
